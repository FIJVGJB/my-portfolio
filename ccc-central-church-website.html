CCC Central Church Website

Professional Collaboration, Minnesota

Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive website for CCC Miracle Parish, featuring over 10 pages with event information, prayer times, staff roles, and interactive features. The project aimed to provide a digital hub for the church community.

Features

  • 10+ pages including events, prayer times, staff profiles, and contact forms.
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile devices.
  • Interactive event calendar and announcements.
  • Content management for staff and service schedules.

My Role

  • Led requirement gathering and client communication.
  • Designed and implemented frontend and backend features.
  • Managed deployment and hosting.
  • Ensured accessibility and user-friendly navigation.

Duration

2 months (Mar 2025 - May 2025)

Outcome

Delivered a robust, scalable website that improved church communication and event management. The project was completed on time, with positive feedback from the client and community.
