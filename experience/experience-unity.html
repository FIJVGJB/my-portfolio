Work Experience

Freelance Unity Developer | Sep 2025 - Dec 2025

I developed a mobile 2D game for a client in Portugal, designed to be a rich open-world experience with interactive NPCs, collectible recipes, and multi-level exploration. The game was built using Unity version 6000.0.58f2 and C# scripting. My main focus was creating smooth navigation, engaging gameplay mechanics, and intuitive player progression.

The game contains four unique levels: Grassland Village, Winter Village, Alcoholic Village, and Candy Cloud Land. Players collect recipes from crates, interact with NPCs using dialogue boxes, and navigate levels via a mini-map. Completing levels unlocks boss fights and advanced stages.

Development included cross-platform optimization for Android, iOS, and Windows. Challenges included ensuring consistent UI scaling, handling touch controls, and managing multiple scenes such as inventory, pause menu, and level transitions. Debugging was crucial to maintain smooth gameplay across different devices.

The gameplay was carefully balanced to offer both challenge and enjoyment. Key mechanics included:

  • Recipe collection and inventory persistence across levels.
  • Turn-based boss fights with respawn mechanics on defeat.
  • Interactive NPC dialogues guiding player progression.
  • Open-world navigation with mini-map support.
  • Level-specific challenges and thematic design for player immersion.

Regular updates and client collaboration were key to success. Feedback emphasized satisfaction with gameplay design, level variety, and smooth controls. The final product is fully functional, immersive, and optimized for multiple platforms.

Project Highlights

  • Four distinct themed levels with unique aesthetics and challenges.
  • Inventory and recipe collection system integrated across levels.
  • Cross-platform performance optimization for Android, iOS, and Windows.
  • Boss fights designed to be challenging yet fair with replayable mechanics.
  • Interactive NPC dialogues and mini-map navigation for player guidance.
  • Positive client feedback and professional freelance delivery.
